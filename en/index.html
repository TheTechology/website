How we work

Four steps. Zero pressure.

From "I'm looking for a car" to keys in hand in about 10 days. No surprises, no hidden fees, no pushy calls. Step-by-step guidance from an ADN Cars representative.

01 · You search

Pick an offer from our online showroom, or tell us the model and must-have features for your next car. We present you with several offers, you choose one, and we take care of the import.

02 · Leasing

We first ask for a set of documents from your company, or personal documents if you'd like leasing as an individual. We forward the documents to a partner leasing company and track the file through to approval.

03 · Transport

After the lease contract is signed and the car is paid for, we schedule platform transport, with insurance, to your address. Our carrier covers most towns across the country.

04 · Delivery

Your new purchase is a reason to celebrate. If the car is a gift, we can help organise a small celebration. Either way, we make sure the moment itself is memorable.
