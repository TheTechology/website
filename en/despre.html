Our story

How it all started.

In the autumn of 2020, Alex bought a car from a car dealer. The car looked perfect in the ad. 2,000 km after the purchase, he discovered a history of two undisclosed accidents, and a string of technical problems followed.

By the end of the year, Alex had launched ADN CARS. The mission was simple: make sure no one else goes through the same thing.

Today we're a team that personally selects every car from Germany. The team doesn't chase sales commissions — our people's income depends on client reviews, not on how many cars we sell in a month.
